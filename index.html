Our Philosophy

At Maki Sushi Rolls, we are the entrepreneurs of sushi handrolls in Ireland, being the first business to bring sushi in a handroll form.

Street Style

We make sushi easy, no need for chopsticks just grab and go. Real food made with real ingredients by real people!

Sustainability

We also strongly believe in sustainability, have recieved an award in 2017 from the sustainable buisness awards, all our packing is biodegrables from Down2Earth Materials

Our Sushi Rolls

We source all of our ingredients within the market itself. We try to use as much local and seasonal produce as possible, changing our menu throughout the year.

Fish

Our fresh fish daily comes from Ballycotton Seafoods and O'Connells Fish Mongers.

Fruit / Vegetables

Our fresh salads, fruit and veg come from Superfruit

Asian

Our Asian supplies such as sushi rice, seaweed and miso come from Mr Bells.

Options

We have a selection of handrolls to choose from daily from traditional raw fish rolls, to cooked fish, chicken and vegan options.

All of our rolls come with a portion of soy sauce and wasabi.
